About Us

My name is Benny Nguyen. I'm 21 years old, and currently a Senior in the Informatics Program at the University of Washington. My interests include mainly things related to health and fitness. However, music is a constant source of enjoyment for me.

Our Goals

At the moment, I believe that the status of our mental health is becoming ignored further and further in our society. This state of decay is terrible, considering the fact that most careers and societal advancements focus more on the "brain" than the "brawn" these days. Music has always been an outlet for me and many others, and I would like to show the impact of music for our mental health. Thus, of one of my goals is to bring awareness to our society's ignorance of mental health through music.
