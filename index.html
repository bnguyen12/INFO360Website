Mission

The mission of this app is to sync up your playlist to different sources according to the mood it gets from you. Using face-detection methods, this app will play bright and cheerful songs when you're happy, or calm and chill tracks when you're stressed. Studies have shown that your mental health can be greatly improved from music, so we aim to help those in trouble as streaming music becomes more widely available!

Goals

Accurate Mood Detection

Ever wanted to understand how you really look like on the outside? Let us do the work. This app indicates if what you think you feel like is what it looks like on the outside!

Latest Songs

The internet has many outlets to listen to your favorite tracks, except it's annoying when you can't find your songs all on the same page. With MoodSongs we put together your favorites for getting things like hype at the gym or relaxed when studying.

Share Your Playlist

We get it, you can share your playlist on Spotify to your friends. Oh, and your YouTube playlist. And your Deezer playlist. Well let us easily compile that for you to share on social media!
